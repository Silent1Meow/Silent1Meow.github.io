ABOUT

Hey there! I am a 3D artist with a focus on making stunning video game art.

I started as a 3D artist in late 2021 when I discovered Source Filmmaker.
I was excited to start using it and was happy with the results but found the limits very quickly.
Shortly after, I transitioned to Blender, where I further honed my skills and pushed my creativity to new heights.

In February of 2022, I discovered Substance Painter and loved the software's ability to create intricate and detailed textures. Since then, it has become an a part of my workflow, and I use it to add depth and realism to retextures of 3D models.

In addition to my passion for 3D art, I am also deeply interested in the technical side of things.
I enjoy learning about new software, tools, and techniques that can enhance my workflow and improve the quality of my work.

I am always pushing myself to experiment with new ideas and approaches, and I am not afraid to take on challenging projects that push the boundaries of what I can do.
Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me and my passion for 3D art. If you have any questions or would like to commission a render, don't hesitate to reach out!
